Training & Placement Cell

Ganga College of Paramedical Science excels in its department of training the students which helps them get hired by several other firms and institutes and give them a chance to explore and prosper in their field of interests. Our training and placement cell excels in its ways to prepare our students for future selections. More than 80% students shows interests to get trained with us and are accordingly placed in leading medical firms operating nationally and inter-nationally as well. This department is being headed by our college and he ensures to organize various meetings and up-coming plans with our tie up institutes and firms to gather requirements for the students up-gradations and training programs. A large number of volunteers are employed to helps us serve our keen interests in placing our students with top hospitals and medical firms. Placement tests, mock interviews, workplace skills and many other procedures are followed to train the students as part of the pre-training modules. Many such pre-recruitment classes are designed to make the students learn the acumen of several issues associated with the medical world research topics. Training and placement cell is continuously working for the upliftment of the students of Ganga College of Paramedical Science under the guidance with his prime focus on designing useful training programs along with developing strong relationship with the recruiting firms and hospitals.
