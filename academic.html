Academic

Academics of Ganga College of Paramedical Sciences is a rare blend of technical skills and traditional emphasis on imparting knowledge and preparing future paramedical science with sharp knowledge and ability to handle the patients. We are constantly working and evolving towards achieving our goal in providing experience based knowledge. We have a full fledged team of experienced doctors and faculties who have excelled in the field of medicines and now training our students to make them capable of exploring every inch of the expanding medical arena. We invite you to explore our services as we are among some of the best pharmacy institutes in Bathinda with highly modernized medical infrastructures. We provide below courses:
  • B.Sc. Operation Theater Technology
  • B.Sc. Radio Medical Imaging Technology
  • B.Sc. Anesthesia Technology
  • Skill Certification in Hospital Administration
  • Skill Certificate Course in Medical Lab Technology
