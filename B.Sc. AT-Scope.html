B.Sc. Anaesthesia Technology: (8 Semester course including Project & Internship)

B.Sc. Anaesthesia Technology is an undergraduate course that specifically concentrates on educating students about the application and scope of Anaesthetic Technology. BSc Anaesthesia colleges in Bangalore, have a curriculum designed to help students to form a strong base in anaesthesia technology to impart clear knowledge on choosing the right an aesthetic for the patient depending upon his/her medical condition. Further, they students shall be trained to operate different types of equipment related to anaesthetic technology. This three-year academic program focuses on lecture-based and practical sessions that help the students understand the fundamentals of operation theatres, intensive care units and emergency wards. An Anaesthetic Technician is a health professional who works as a part of the operation team alongside the anaesthesiologist to sedate and properly anaesthetizes to prepare a patient for surgery. They students shall also assigned to ensure the proper functioning of anaesthesia equipment during the surgical procedures. They ensure safe and efficient anaesthesia care by monitoring the dosages of medications given to patients. Since anaesthetic procedures involve great risks, some soft skills like attention to detail, problem-solving skills, and ability to work under stress are desired. A clear knowledge enables students to ensure the painless performance of medical procedures that would otherwise cause pain to the patient.

BSc Anaesthesia Technology course is taken by candidates who wish to pursue a career as an allied health professional. This course gives them the knowledge to use an aesthesia equipment as well as anaesthetic agents. The education gained by the students in Anaesthesia Technology degree helps them in choosing the correct anaesthetic treatment for a patient. The key responsibilities and duties of an anaesthetic technician are:

  • Operating and monitoring anesthetic equipment in the operating room.
  • Properly setting and analyzing the results in the equipment before, during and after surgery.
  • Managing the supply inventory of operation theatre/room.
  • Preparing anaesthetic drugs under the supervision of a qualified anesthesiologist.
  • Managing equipment that monitors the vital signs of the patient during a surgery.
  • Helping in IVs and airway devices.
  • Securing and establishing an airway.
  • Helping to transfer patients to post-operative care units/wards.

Anesthesia Technologist Program - Scope

There are many career prospects for BSc Anaesthesia Technology graduates. Top healthcare institutions and hospitals hire them to high paid jobs, depending upon their level of proficiency. Some of the career options for Anaesthesia Technologist programs are Anaesthesia Technician, Paediatrician Medical Consultant, Assistant Clinical Associate, Certified and Registered Nurse Anaesthetist and Associate Consultant. The specializations available are Cardiothoracic Anaesthetists, General Anaesthetics, and Intensive care, Neuro Anaesthetists, Paediatric Anaesthetic and Obstetric Anaesthesia. The higher education courses available after graduation in anaesthesia technology are M.Sc. in Anaesthesia Technology, M.Sc. in Operation Theatre, M.Sc. in Anaesthesia and Operation Theatre Techniques, and more.


Anaesthesia Tech Job

Anaesthesia Technology jobs are the most practiced career among anaesthesia technology graduates. They monitor the stock and supply of anaesthetic drugs, equipment setup, and take care of the patients. CCUs, ICUs, Surgery wards, Emergency departments, Operation Theatres and Surgery wards are the common workplaces for an anaesthetic technician. Most of them earn different certifications to improve their career prospects. They generally work with a team of Surgeons, anaesthesiologist and nursing staff. Qualified anaesthesia technicians are hired in both government and private hospitals and clinics. The pay scale slightly varies between government and private sector jobs.
The annual average pay of a beginner in anaesthetic tech jobs ranges between 3 lakhs to 4 lakhs.


Program Outcome

  • Practice within the scope of the professional standards of Anaesthesia technology.

  • Practice all anaesthesia procedures following ethical and legal standards.
  • Uphold safety, privacy and cultural differences of patients.
  • Demonstrates dedication, reliability.
