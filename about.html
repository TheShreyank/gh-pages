ABOUT US

GANGA COLLEGE is one such institution aimed at attracting people who would like to make the best of this excellent opportunity. The institute focuses to deliver high quality, comprehensive, integrated pharmacy education including innovative thoughts towards modern technology and research.


The institute is supported by full-fledged high tech amenities and well trained, highly qualified teaching faculties. We prepare the students for the lives of expanding opportunity, service and achievement. As an independent, comprehensive college, it offers students of different backgrounds with purposeful learning for the whole of life.
